Tohol : How to install ?

Tohol is a powerful Telegram bot designed to be an emergency response tool.

Whether you're in a tough situation or just need help fast, Tohol will notify all Telegram users within a specified radius of your location, ensuring help is just around the corner.

Please follow these steps to install:

  • (If you do not have the Telegram app), please install the Telegram app.

  • Scan this QR code or Click on this button

  • Immediately after scaning QR or clicking on the link, Tohol bot will be initialized in telegram app on your phone. Click on "start" button and follow instructions to complete registration.

Tohol : How to register ?

  • Enter your name, location and phone number to register.

  • Next, if you want to receive alerts from nearby Tohol users, then select "Yes".
    Otherwise, select "No".

  • Your registration is now complete. Unless you need to send an alert right now, you can close Telegram Tohol bot window. You don't need to press the report button.

Tohol : How to use it ?

  • In case of an emergency, to send alert to nearby Tohol users, click the report button.

  • Select incident type. For example, burglary, robbery, extortion, etc.

  • Next share your location by clicking on the "Location Sharing" button at the bottom

  • Your report will be submitted and the volunteers or users who selected to get alert, will receive an alert about your emergency and they'll also receieve your location info, contact number and name

Tohol : How does it work ?

When you trigger an alert using Tohol, nearby volunteers/quick response teams will receive your name, phone number and location information with an alert. They'll try to attend your location as soon as possible. quickly.

Please note that Tohol is completely community driven. It's not associated with any private or public organization.

Tohol : Who can be the volunteer?

Tohol doesn't have any specific requirement to become a volunteer. If you choose to receive alerts from nearby locations, you will receive alerts when nearby incidents happens. You only need the passion to help your neighbours and community.
