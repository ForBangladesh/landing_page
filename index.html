Tohol : How to install?

Tohol is a powerful Telegram bot designed to be an emergency response tool.

Whether you're in a tough situation or just need help fast, Tohol will notify all Telegram users within a specified radius of your location, ensuring help is just around the corner.

Please follow these steps to install:

  • One. (If you do not have the Telegram app), please install the Telegram app.

  • Two. Scan this QR code or click on this link.

  • Three. Join the Tohol Telegram group, and the bot will be activated in your inbox.

Tohol : How does it work?

When you trigger an alert using Tohol, near volunteers/quick response team will get your name, number and location information. They'll try to attend your location quickly.

Tohol : Who can be the volunteers?

Tohol doesn't have any specific requirement to become a volunteer. Anyone can choose to be a volunteer during registration. In that case, the person will get alert when an incident happens.
